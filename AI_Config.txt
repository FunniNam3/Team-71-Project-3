## 🧑‍🍳 Inventory Awareness (AI Rules)

### ⚠️ Low Stock Threshold

* If `amount ≤ 5` → mark as **LOW STOCK**
* If `amount = 0` → mark as **OUT OF STOCK** and:

  * Disable menu items using that ingredient
  * Notify staff

### 🚨 Critical Items

* Tapioca pearls
* Milk (all types)
* Tea leaves
* Syrups

### 🤖 AI Behavior

* Suggest alternatives if item unavailable
* Example:

  * No tapioca → suggest popping boba
  * No whole milk → suggest oat/almond milk

### 📈 Sales Reports

**Generate automatically:**

* Every shift change (X Report optional)

### 🧾 Z Report (End of Day)

Generate at:

* **Closing time (9PM–12AM depending on day)**

Includes:

* Total sales
* Number of transactions
* Most popular items
* Discounts used
* Payment breakdown (cash/card/online)

---

### 🧾 X Report (Mid-Day / Shift Report)

Generate:

* At shift changes OR every 4–6 hours

Includes:

* Current sales total
* Orders in progress
* Inventory usage snapshot
